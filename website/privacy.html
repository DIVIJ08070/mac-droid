Bifrost

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

The short version: Bifrost collects nothing. There are no servers, no account, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. Everything the app does happens directly between your own Mac and your own Android phone, over your local network — and it never leaves your devices.

What we collect

Nothing. Bifrost has no backend and no user accounts. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. We cannot see your clipboard, files, messages, notifications, screen, audio, calls, or contacts — none of it reaches us, because there is no "us" in the connection. The app is a direct link between two devices you own.

How your data moves

When you pair your phone and Mac, they connect directly to each other over your own Wi-Fi, the phone's hotspot, a USB cable, or a private VPN of your choosing. The connection is encrypted end-to-end (ECDH P-256 key exchange, AES-256-GCM). No third party is involved and nothing is routed through any external service.

Permissions, and why the app asks

Bifrost requests some sensitive Android permissions so its features can work. Every one is used only on your device, only for the feature you turn on, and only to move that data to your own paired Mac — never anywhere else:

  • Notification access — to mirror your phone's notifications onto your Mac and let you reply from there.
  • Microphone — to stream your phone's mic to your Mac when you start that feature.
  • Screen capture — to mirror your phone's screen to your Mac when you start it.
  • Accessibility — to let your Mac control the phone (trackpad, remote control) and to sync the current browser tab. It acts only on the input you send and reads nothing else.
  • Files & media — to send, receive, and sync the files and photos you choose.
  • Phone & call state, contacts — if you enable the Calls feature, to show incoming calls on your Mac and let you manage them. Caller names come from your on-device contacts and are shown only on your paired Mac.

You grant each permission individually, and any feature works only while you have it enabled.

Third parties

None. Bifrost contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party trackers.

Children

Bifrost is a general-purpose utility and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date above.
